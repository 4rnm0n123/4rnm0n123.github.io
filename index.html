Home

This Minecraft world is a visual recreation of the Macbeth world, with each area being decorated to represent a specific theme. Instead of telling the story, the world uses visual symbolism and quotes to show themes and give analysis. In the world, we explored themes such as fierceness, ambitiousness, foolishness, and traitorousness. Each specific area is designed to illustrate a theme; the location of where each theme is represented is based off events in the play. Along with this, signs are scattered around each themed area with carefully selected quotations which show the chosen theme.

By combining physical representations with original lines from the play, the design helps to visualize the play and its events. The goal is to encourage viewers to see what Shakespeare’s themes look like in visual and interactive experiences, and to help them understand without explicit narration.
